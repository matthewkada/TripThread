TripThread Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

At TripThread, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

Quick Summary: We collect minimal information to provide social networking features. Your data is securely stored on Firebase. You can delete your account and data at any time. We never sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Apple ID: Email address and name (via Sign in with Apple)
  • Profile Data: Username, profile photo, bio, travel preferences

User-Generated Content

  • Threads & Posts: Text content, tags, hashtags
  • Media: Photos and videos you upload
  • Messages: Direct message content
  • Events: Event details you create (date, time, location)

Technical Data

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used
  • Location: General location (only when you share it)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain TripThread's social networking features
  • Enable connections between travelers at the same destination
  • Allow you to share content with other users
  • Send notifications about relevant activity (likes, follows, replies)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Process premium subscriptions (if you choose to upgrade)
  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries

3. Data Storage & Third-Party Services

TripThread uses Firebase (Google) as our cloud infrastructure provider:

Firebase Authentication

  • Manages Sign in with Apple
  • Stores secure session tokens
  • Handles user authentication

Firebase Firestore

  • Stores user profiles and settings
  • Hosts threads, posts, and content
  • Enables real-time updates

Firebase Storage

  • Stores photos and videos you upload
  • Hosts profile images
  • Provides secure media access

Firebase processes data according to their Privacy Policy. We do not access or share your data with any other third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure Apple Sign In integration
  • Access Control: Only authenticated users can access content
  • Regular Updates: We monitor and update security practices
Important: While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your personal data through the app
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data permanently
  • Export: Request a copy of your data (contact support)
  • Opt Out: Disable notifications in device settings
  • Complain: Report privacy concerns to us or relevant authorities

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open TripThread
  2. Go to your Profile
  3. Tap Settings
  4. Select "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm deletion

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed from our servers within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

TripThread is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it immediately.

7. International Users

TripThread is based in the United States. If you use TripThread from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers are located.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will:

  • Revise the "Last Updated" date
  • Notify users through the app for significant changes
  • Obtain consent when required by law

Your continued use of TripThread after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: support@tripthread.app
  • Website: https://tripthread.app

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days of receipt.
